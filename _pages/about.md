My name is Tyler Kerns. I started this site initially to document my process as I attempt a career change into IT. I quickly realized that not only will this serve as a reference for myself, but it may also help anyone interested in following some of the same steps that I did. I am a current Computer Science student, graduating in December of 2020 with an Associate of Science. After obtaining that degree, I will be pursuing a Bachelor of Science in IT. I am currently at the first stages of a career change into IT from my current role as a paramedic in Minneapolis and I have grown more passionate about IT with every new topic I encounter.

While most content found here will be in the form of blog posts, I will be maintaining an organized page of resources that I have utilized along the way.

Any questions or comments may be directed to myself: tyler@tylerkerns.com
